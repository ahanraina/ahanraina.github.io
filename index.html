Ahan Raina

I am a writer based in New Jersey who hopes to build a career discussing issues of philosophy, culture, politics, and policy. I was educated at the University of Chicago, with degrees in Philosophy and in Law, Letters, and Society, the latter with a focus field in Law and Economics. I have till now mostly written for the Harper Review, and done research on moral philosophy, constitutional law, the philosophy of language, and Ludwig Wittgenstein.
